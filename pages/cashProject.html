Cash

Discord Bot

Actively Improving

Summary

Cash is a custom-built bot designed to simplify and streamline community management on Discord. With its powerful automation features, role organization, and activity tracking capabilities, Cash makes managing a server a breeze.

It’s highly customizable to suit the unique needs of every community, ensuring a smooth, efficient experience for both server admins and members.

As of 2025, Cash actively manages a thriving Discord community of over 1,000 users, offering built-in security features to keep the server safe and secure.

Role

As the sole developer of Cash, I am responsible for every aspect of the bot’s design, development, and deployment.

From implementing core features like automation, role management, and activity tracking, to ensuring the bot is secure and continuously improving based on user feedback, my role has been essential in shaping Cash into the tool it is today.

My expertise in Node.js, MongoDB, and Discord.js has driven the project forward, ensuring it remains efficient and user-friendly.

Technologies
